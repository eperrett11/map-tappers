How Map Tappers Works

Scores are scraped automatically via a script from the group chat at the end of each day.

The main leaderboard is just for the current month. At the end of the month, whoever's on top is that month's winner — then the board starts fresh the next month.

Your leaderboard rank is the average of your best ten rounds for the month.

You need to log at least ten rounds in a month to appear on the board. Until you hit ten, you'll sit at 0 with a "not enough rounds yet" note.

Because only your ten best rounds count, any extra scores beyond those can't drag your rank down.

Past Months shows the top 3 finishers for any earlier month.

A win means finishing 1st at the end of a month. Wins are counted in the Trophy Cabinet and the Compare Players table — one trophy for each month you've topped the board.
